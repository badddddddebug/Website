Instructor’s Presence: Room-Based Attendance and Request System

The Instructor’s Presence: Room-Based Attendance and Request System is a technological solution designed to enhance the operational efficiency of educational institutions.

1

CORE

FUNCTIONALITIES

Campus Presence Monitoring

By using RFID technology, the system allows authorized personnel such as deans and administrators to determine an instructor's current location within the campus. This feature helps improve coordination and ensures that instructors are available for classes, meetings, or other duties as needed.

Instructor Attendance Monitoring

The system accurately tracks and records the attendance of instructors as they enter or exit designated rooms, such as classrooms or computer laboratories. This feature ensures reliable documentation of instructor attendance for administrative purposes.

Room Request Management

The system provides instructors with the capability to request specific rooms for their classes or activities directly through the platform. This streamlines the process of room allocation, reduces booking errors, and improves overall scheduling efficiency.
